Jack Ma

  • Jack Ma was born on September 10 th, 1964 as Ma Yun (Chinese: 马云)
  • He was chinese businessman, founder and executive chairman of Alibaba Group.
  • Alibaba Group is family of successful internet-based businesses.
  • He is the richest man in technology in China.
  • Jack is the first Chinese that appeared on the cover of the Forbes.
  • Ma learned english as a kid by giving tour guides to the tourists in exchange for english lessons.
  • He even got the nickname Jack from one of the tourists that was hard to pronounce his real name.
  • Jack Ma had series of failures during his life, he was rejected from Harvard 10 times, from his college in China 3 times and job in KFC and he is one of the most successful people in the world.
  • In 1995. he was first introduced to the Internet while visiting his friend in America. First word he had typed in was "beer".
  • His second search was general information about China and the fact that there were no many results caused Ma to build his first website and realise that Internet has a lot to offer.
